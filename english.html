Coming to Zagreb? We are offering accomodation for you!

Apartments Dekovic are situated in center of Zagreb, in quiet and peacefull old part of city. From our house, it's only 10 minutes to downtown center. We offer private accomodation for very reasonable prices. Our clients profile is mainly tourist or business visitors seeking good value/price ratio.

People usually prefer this kind of accomodation because it's more flexible and less expansive. In contrary to hotel services, we can arrange various services (as wireless internet access, washing and cleaning, or you can even your take your pet with you) with minimum charge. If you are looking for a place to stay while visiting Zagreb, and you want quiet and clean place to sleep and rest after a long day out - this is it.

Our accomodation

We are offering accomodation in apartment or single room with bathroom. Each unit has separate entrance, so you can have your privacy. Price is around 170 kn (23 €) per person daily.

Wireless internet access is free for our guests, that way you don't have to concern yourself with expensive mobile internet while staying in Zagreb.

Location
